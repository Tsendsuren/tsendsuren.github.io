About Me

I am an experimental physicist with a focus on attosecond physics. With extensive expertise in cutting-edge CEP-stabilized femtosecond technology, I excel in generating single-cycle optical pulses through precise post-compression techniques.

My skills extend to the construction and optimization of attosecond beamlines, where I harness the power of advanced instrumentation to explore the fundamental dynamics of atoms and molecules on ultrafast timescales.

I thrive on the challenges of pushing the boundaries of ultrafast science and am committed to advancing our understanding of fundamental physical processes at the shortest timescales imaginable. Let's connect to explore potential collaborations!
