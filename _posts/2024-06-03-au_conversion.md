## Atomic unit convention in ultrafast physics


### Laser intensity conversion

```python
Intensity [au] = Intensity [W.cm2] * 3.51e-16 
```

### Energy conversion
```python
Energy [au] = Energy [eV] / 27.21
```

### Time conversion
```python
t [au] = t [fs] * 1000 / 24.2
```

### Wavelength conversion
```python
lambda [au] = lambda [nm] * 18.89
```
